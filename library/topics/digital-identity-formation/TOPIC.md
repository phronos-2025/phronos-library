# Digital Identity Formation and Expression

## Overview

This topic explores how individuals form and express their identity through technology, with a focus on conversational AI interactions. The research questions are organized into eight thematic clusters addressing theoretical foundations, linguistic markers, behavioral predictors, methodological considerations, and ethical frameworks.
